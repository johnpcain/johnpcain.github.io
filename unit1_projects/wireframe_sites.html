MarketWatch

What problem is the web page solving? The page solves the need for timlely, relevant financial news in a succint format.
What visual challenges are there?

  1. How do you present financial news in a way that is engaging and stimulating?
  2. How do you link to the many different offerings of the parents site without the page appearing too cluttered?
  3. How do you group together news within different categories without requiring the user to over-navigate?
